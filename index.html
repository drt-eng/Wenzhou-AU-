About the Wenzhou African Union

Our Story

Founded in 2022, the Wenzhou African Union (WAU) was born from the shared vision of African students, professionals, and entrepreneurs living in Wenzhou, Zhejiang Province, China.

What started as informal gatherings among fellow Africans has grown into a structured, impactful organization that represents the interests and celebrates the achievements of every African in this vibrant Chinese city.

We are more than an organization — we are a family. A family that spans the entire African continent, from the deserts of the Sahara to the shores of the Cape of Good Hope, all united in one city on the other side of the world.

WAU serves as the bridge between our rich African heritage and our life in China, ensuring that no African in Wenzhou ever feels alone, unsupported, or forgotten.

🌟 What We Do

  • ✅Cultural Celebrations — Africa Day, independence days, cultural festivals
  • ✅Community Support — Welfare, integration assistance, emergency support
  • ✅Business Networking — Connecting African entrepreneurs and businesses
  • ✅Sports & Recreation — AFCON tournament, sports events, fitness
  • ✅Awards & Recognition — Annual Africa Day Awards ceremony
  • ✅Cultural Exchange — Building bridges with our Chinese hosts
📅

About Africa Day

Africa Day, celebrated annually on May 25th, commemorates the founding of the Organisation of African Unity (now the African Union) on May 25, 1963, in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia.

It was on this historic day that 32 African leaders came together to form a unified body dedicated to the liberation, unity, and progress of the African continent.

Today, Africa Day is celebrated across all 54 African nations and by African communities worldwide — including right here in Wenzhou, China! 🇨🇳🤝🌍

🎯 Why We Celebrate

  • 🏛️Honor the founding of African unity
  • 🎭Celebrate African culture, art, and music
  • 🤝Strengthen bonds within our community
  • 🏆Recognize African excellence abroad
  • 🌉Build bridges between Africa and China
  • 🍲Share our food, music, and traditions

Our Vision & Mission

👁️

Our Vision

A thriving, united, and empowered African community in Wenzhou where every member — regardless of nationality, language, or background — has the opportunity to succeed, connect, and celebrate our rich cultural heritage.

We envision a community where:

→Every African in Wenzhou feels they have a home away from home
→African businesses thrive and grow through community support
→Our children know and celebrate their roots
→Strong bridges of friendship and cooperation exist with our Chinese hosts
→African talent, innovation, and excellence are recognized and celebrated
🎯

Our Mission

The Wenzhou African Union exists to unite, empower, and celebrate the African community in Wenzhou, China through the following pillars:
1Cultural Exchange: Organizing events that showcase African heritage
2Community Welfare: Providing support, assistance, and advocacy
3Business Development: Supporting African entrepreneurs
4Recognition: Celebrating African achievements through annual awards
5Unity: Bringing together all 54 African nations under one banner
